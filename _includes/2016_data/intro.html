The 15th European Workshop on Reinforcement Learning

Dates:    September 2022
Location:  Milan, Italy

Description

The 15th European workshop on reinforcement learning (EWRL 2020) invites reinforcement learning researchers to participate in the revival of this world class event. We plan to make this an exciting event for researchers worldwide, not only for the presentation of top quality papers, but also as a forum for ample discussion of open problems and future research directions.

Reinforcement learning is an active field of research which deals with the problem of sequential decision making in unknown (and often) stochastic and/or partially observable environments. Recently there has been a wealth of both impressive empirical results, as well as significant theoretical advances. Both types of advances are of significant importance and we would like to create a forum to discuss such interesting results.

The workshop will cover a range of sub-topics including (but not limited to):

  • Exploration-Exploitation in RL
  • Policy search/optimization methods
  • Partially observable RL
  • Multi-agent RL
  • Multi-objective RL
  • Non-stationary RL
  • Risk-sensitive and robust RL
  • RL in applications/Real-life RL
  • Deep RL
  • Tranfer/multi-task/meta-RL
  • Safe RL
  • Hierarchical RL
  • Lifelong/continual RL
  • Imitation Learning and Inverse RL
  • Online learning and Multi-armed Bandits

Paper Submission

We invite submissions for the 15th European Workshop on Reinforcement Learning (EWRL 2020) from the entire reinforcement learning spectrum. Authors can submit a 2-6 pages paper in JMLR format (excluding references) that will be reviewed by the program committee in a double-blind procedure. The papers can present new work or give a summary of recent work of the author(s). All papers will be considered for the poster sessions. Outstanding long papers (4-6 pages) will also be considered for a 20 minutes oral presentation. One paper will be selected for the best paper award. There will be no proceedings of EWRL15.
